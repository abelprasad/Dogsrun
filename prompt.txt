Redesign the entire DOGSRUN frontend to match a cohesive 3-tone theme consistently across all pages.

## Design System

### Colors
- Dark nav/footer: #111 (near-black)
- Hero/accent backgrounds: #fffbeb (amber-tinted cream)
- Body/cards: #ffffff (white)
- Accent: #f59e0b (amber) — CTAs, highlights, mark tags, badges only
- Accent text on amber: #451a03
- Body text: #111 (headings), #6b7280 (body), #9ca3af (muted/nav links)
- Borders: #e5e7eb (default), #d1d5db (inputs/buttons)

### Typography
- Headings: font-weight 900, tracking-tight, color #111
- The word "run" or key brand word on hero pages: wrapped in <mark style="background:#f59e0b; color:#111; padding:0 4px; border-radius:3px; font-style:normal;">
- Nav wordmark: font-weight 600, letter-spacing wider
- Body: text-sm or text-base, color #6b7280, line-height relaxed

### Nav (appears on every page)
- Background: #111
- Logo: amber circle + "DOGSRUN" in white
- Links: #9ca3af
- Register button: bg #f59e0b, text #451a03, font-weight 600, rounded-lg
- Login: #9ca3af plain link

### Cards
- bg white, border border-gray-100, rounded-xl, shadow-none, p-6
- Headings: font-weight 700, color #111
- Body: text-sm, color #6b7280

### Buttons
- Primary: bg #111, text white, font-weight 600, rounded-lg, px-5 py-2.5
- Accent: bg #f59e0b, text #451a03, font-weight 600, rounded-lg, px-5 py-2.5
- Ghost: border border-gray-300, text #374151, bg transparent, rounded-lg, px-5 py-2.5

### Status badges
- Available: bg #dcfce7, text #166534
- Pending: bg #fef3c7, text #92400e
- Adopted: bg #e0e7ff, text #3730a3
- Deceased/transferred: bg #f3f4f6, text #6b7280

### Page structure pattern
Every page should follow this band pattern:
1. Dark nav (#111) — shared component
2. Page hero band (#fffbeb, border-bottom border-gray-200) — page title + short description, py-12 px-8
3. White content body — cards, forms, tables, py-8 px-8
