### BUG 2: Rescue Alerts section showing "0 alerts sent" even when alerts exist
The alerts query uses:
.select('*, organizations!alerts_rescue_id_fkey(name, email, city, state)')
This join may be failing silently. Change it to a simpler select without the foreign key hint:
.select('*, organizations(name, email, city, state)')
If that also fails, do two separate queries: first fetch alerts for the dog, then fetch org names by rescue_id.

Also the section is only shown when `isDogShelter === true`. Make sure this condition is correctly evaluating — log userOrg.id and dog.shelter_id to verify they match.
